KAMSA- KDRAMA FESTIVAL

A Design Project for An Imaginative Music Festival

Live Your K-drama Moment

The KAMSA Festival is a K-drama Festival that wil be held in the Los Angeles Convention Center in the spring. The word, KAMSA is derived from the word "thank you" in Korean, which also means grateful. As a brand, we are grateful that K-dramas are a part of our lives. K-dramas are an essential part of Korean culture and is loved by fans worldwide. The festival features a small business corner as well as many fun activites, filled with all things K-drama related. The main event is a concert that contains OSTs (Original Soundtrack) from famous singers and idols from the K-dramas.
