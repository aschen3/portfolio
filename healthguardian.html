HEALTHGUARDIAN

UI/UX Designer

Everyone should be stress-free with their medical financials.
View Final Prototype

HealthGuardian is a platform intended for hospital patients who require assistance with their medical financial needs. This app caters to patients who are low-income or use public health insurance companies to get more information about their medical finances. "Bandy" is HealthGuardian’s helper who guides and educates their users about medical financial terms. With this app, patients can easily pay off their medical bills in one centralized location, and access a dedicated page for financial resources to assist with their medical expenses.

After experiencing a stressful situation for an unexpected situation, I am hoping people will no longer deal with the hassle of finding resources to help alone. There’s a lot I need to improve on this app, but it is a start to help relieve those who went through the same experience. Although there are only a few health insurance companies on this app, I hope to expand to different providers and hospitals.
