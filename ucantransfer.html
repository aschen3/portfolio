U CAN TRANSFER

UI/UX Designer

A centralized place to make the UC transfer process simple and achievable.
View Homepage

For this project, I created a professional informational website (homepage only) called U CAN TRANSFER.

Upon transferring from a community college or junior college, a common issue is the "transfer process." Many students are overwhelmed with the amount of information and deadlines, when it comes to the transfer preparation. This website provides resources and offers step-by-step guides on transferring to a University of California (UC) campus for community college students.
