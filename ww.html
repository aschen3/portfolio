WindWomen of NorthWind Dragonboat

Jersey Designer

Established in 2014, Northwind Dragonboat is a competitive dragon boat team located in San Francisco. The team competes in both national and international races every year with the 2024 Club Crew World Championships (CCWC) being their most recent race. Their official women's crew, "Windwomen," was created in 2023 and debuted in the 2024 Arizona Dragon Boat Race in Tempe.

V.1

My design featured a softer color palette with shades of lilac, turquoise, and pink, compared to other jerseys that featured bold colors and patterns.I had the honors of designing the race jersey for the women's crew. I learned a lot about creating clothing templates on Adobe Illustrator and was excited to see my design come to life.
