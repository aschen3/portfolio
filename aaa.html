Asian American Association at UC Davis

Graphic Design Chair, Intern

The Asian American Association (AAA) at UC Davis is a student-run club that promotes unity among Asian communities. AAA strives to establish a safe and nurturing space on the UC Davis campus that promotes awareness and involvement in the Asian American community on the campus.

Starting out as a Graphic Design Intern in Winter 2023, I collaborated with the AAA Graphic Design Chairs in creating social media graphics. Upon the next academic year (2023-2024), I applied to be a Graphic Design Chair. This position allowed me to experiment with my art style, color palettes, and typography. As Graphic Design Chair, my responsibilities were creating promotional materials, ranging from general meetings, educational graphics, and events for the club's social media platforms.
