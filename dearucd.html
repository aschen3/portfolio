Dear UC Davis

Dear UC Davis was a project I made for my Interactive Media class in Summer 2024. In that summer, I learned HTML and CSS within six weeks. I decided to create a digital scrapbook of my two years at UC Davis. Since I transferred to Davis in the Fall of 2022, I have a lot of memories of being here and living out my college experience. I took many photos and made lifelong friends and will use them for my scrapbook. Dear UC Davis can be seen on both desktop and mobile, therefore viewers can look on either device.
