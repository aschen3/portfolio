REFRESH

Graphic Design and Packaging for an Imaginative Beverage Company

Instead of dreading on drinking an elixr from a night out of drinking, Refresh is a beverage company that creates recovery drinks that implements classic Hawaiian fruit flavors. The recovery drinks have electrolytes and probiotics that will leave you feeling rejuvenated and ready to start your day.

For this project, I believe I improved at creating patterns and mockups. The logo was my biggest challenge, but it turned out better than I imagined.
